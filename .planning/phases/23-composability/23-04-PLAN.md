<objective>
Retrofit the generator's collision-detection machinery to implement the broad overload rule locked in 23-CONTEXT.md: two fluent methods are a collision ONLY when their names AND parameter-type sequences are both identical. Signature-distinct same-name methods emit as C# overloads; ambiguity at call sites is left to the C# compiler. CVJG0052 is narrowed accordingly; its message updated; other collision diagnostics across the generator audited and (per audit) narrowed, message-updated, or left unchanged. Produce the audit disposition table in the plan SUMMARY.

Purpose: Enables Model D's incidental overloads (e.g., `[MultipleFluentMethods]` template expansion producing multiple signature-distinct bulk methods on the same accumulator step) to coexist on the same step, and generalizes the policy generator-wide â€” "be as flexible as the language already is" (23-CONTEXT.md).

Output: Narrowed collision predicates in `TargetContextFilter`, potentially-extended `FluentMethodSignatureEqualityComparer` (modifier-tracking), CVJG0052 message update + release-note annotation, updated `AccumulatorNameCollisionTests`, new `CollectionMethodOverloadingTests` suite, and a retrofit audit table in the SUMMARY.
</objective>
